# QR Code Generator for Development

Generate QR codes to access development pages from your mobile device.

Run: `node scripts/qr-gen.js [page]`

Available commands:
- `node scripts/qr-gen.js` - Show all available pages
- `node scripts/qr-gen.js templates` - Generate QR for Templates page
- `node scripts/qr-gen.js feed` - Generate QR for Feed page
- `node scripts/qr-gen.js upload` - Generate QR for Upload page
- `node scripts/qr-gen.js edit-look` - Generate QR for Edit Look page
- `node scripts/qr-gen.js create-post` - Generate QR for Create Post page
- `node scripts/qr-gen.js profile` - Generate QR for Profile page
- `node scripts/qr-gen.js admin` - Generate QR for Admin page
- `node scripts/qr-gen.js all` - Generate QR codes for all pages
- `node scripts/qr-gen.js list` - List all available pages
